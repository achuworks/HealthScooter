Health Management System (HMS)

About Us

The Health Management System (HMS) is an advanced web-based platform that revolutionizes patient care through seamless integration of technology and healthcare services.

Our Features

Dual-Login Interface

Accommodates both patients and healthcare providers, fostering a collaborative care environment.

Patient Portal

Patients can manage their medical records, book appointments, and engage with healthcare services through a secure portal.

Healthcare Provider Tools

Offers robust access to and updating of patient information via forms completed by medical staff.

Firebase Realtime Database

Ensures instantaneous synchronization of data across the network for up-to-date patient records.

Intelligent Chatbot

Acts as a first point of contact for patients, addressing inquiries and aiding in the appointment booking process.

Secure and Scalable

Utilizes Firebase for authentication and database functionalities, ensuring data security, privacy compliance, and scalability.
