Healthy Diet

Recommendations and tips to improve nutrition

01

Includes a variety of foods

Be sure to include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and low-fat dairy in your daily diet. This will provide you with the necessary nutrients to maintain your health.

02

Control the portions

It is important to eat adequate portions to avoid excess calories. Use smaller plates, read food labels, and familiarize yourself with the recommended servings for each type of food.

03

Drink enough water

Stay hydrated by drinking enough water throughout the day. Avoid sugary drinks and opt for water, tea, or unsweetened infusions.

04

Limit consumption of processed foods

Highly processed foods are typically high in saturated fat, added sugars, and sodium. Try to limit your consumption and opt for fresh and natural foods whenever possible.

05

Reduce the consumption of sugar and salt

Excess sugar and salt in the diet can be harmful to health. Limit consumption of sugary foods and drinks, as well as processed foods that contain high levels of salt.
